Personal Profile

Matej Navara is an aspiring game developer based in London. Recently he graduated with a BSc Hons in Computer Science (Games) from the University of Brighton. During this time he studied a wide range of subject areas from web development to artificial intelligence, all while maintaining various jobs outside of university in different industries. During the third year he created a small development studio with three other students to form Greedy Gull Studios (www.GreedyGull.co.uk).

Now after graduating and taking a well deserved few months travelling Matej is eager to begin his journey into the industry he has sought after since childhood. Although not yet specialized in a particular field he is eager to continue learning and spends much of his free time developing skills in key applications and programming languages.
